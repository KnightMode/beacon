No Slack workspace yet? Set one up in about two minutes
  1. Download the Slack app for macOS.
  2. Open the downloaded file and drag Slack into your Applications folder.
  3. Launch Slack and choose Create a new workspace.
  4. Use your work email, name the workspace, and invite your team.
  5. Come back to this tab and hit Connect Slack above.
